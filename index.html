About Me:

Hello, my name is Jennifer Vossman. I graduated from the Univeristy of Kansas with a degree in Elementary Education in 2016. After teaching 5th grade for two years, I had a career change when I joined the 435 Magazine team as a Digital Editor. During this time, I found my love of technology and decided to further my education at the KUf Coding Bootcamp. I think of myself as a life long learner, and always persue opportunities for personal growth.

Aside from this, I also utilize my free time to teach students in the mornings. I am an ESL (English as a Second Language) teacher through a program called VIPKid where I have the opportunity to teach students online in China. I also volunteer as the social media coordinator at a local pet rescue, Lucky 13 Rescue. I am extremely passionate about working with others, using my creativity, and pursuing my goals start to finish.
